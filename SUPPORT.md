**Email**: [amworkspace@outlook.com](mailto:amworkspace@outlook.com)

When reporting a bug, please include:

- Device model (e.g. iPhone 14 Pro Max, Pixel 7)
- OS version (e.g. iOS 18.2, Android 14)
- App version (Settings → About)
- Steps to reproduce the problem
- Screenshots if relevant

We usually reply within 2 business days.

## Common questions

### Prayer times look wrong for my location

1. Open the app → Settings → Calculation Method
2. Pick the method used by your local mosque (ISNA, MWL, Umm al-Qura, Karachi, etc.)
3. Make sure location permission is granted and your device has an accurate GPS fix

### Qibla compass is spinning or inaccurate

- Move away from metal objects, speakers, and magnets
- Calibrate your phone's compass: open Apple/Google Maps and tilt your phone in a figure-8 motion
- On iPhone, enable **Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Compass Calibration**

### Audio recitation won't play

- Check your internet connection (recitations stream from everyayah.com)
- Try a different reciter from the player controls
- Restart the app if audio gets stuck

### How do I back up my bookmarks?

Bookmarks are stored locally on your device. Cross-device sync is on the roadmap for a future release.
