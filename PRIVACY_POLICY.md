# Privacy Policy

**Last updated**: April 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Quran app ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information.

## Summary

The App is privacy-first. All data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

## Data we access

### Location (optional)
The App requests your device's approximate location to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. This data is:
- Used only on your device to perform calculations
- Never transmitted to our servers (we don't have any)
- Never shared with third parties
- Discarded when the app is closed; only the resulting prayer times are cached locally

If you deny location permission, the App falls back to Makkah coordinates.

### Local storage
The App stores the following on your device:
- Quran text and translations (bundled, read-only)
- Bookmarks and last-read position
- Settings (calculation method, reciter, theme, etc.)
- Tasbeeh counter state

Uninstalling the App removes all of this data.

## Data we do NOT collect

- Names, email addresses, or other personal identifiers
- Device identifiers (advertising ID, IMEI, etc.)
- Usage analytics or crash reports
- Contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or any other device data
- Payment information (the App is free)

## Third-party services

The App makes one type of network request:
- Loading external YouTube live streams (Makkah Live, Madinah Live) when you tap those tiles. These are subject to YouTube's privacy policy.

The App does not integrate any analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.

## Children's privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Any changes will appear in this document with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
